Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to my crochet shop! Here are some answers to common questions I get from our lovely customers. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out!

1. What types of crochet items do you sell?

We offer a wide variety of handmade crochet pieces including scarves, blankets, hats, accessories and more! Each item is carefully crafted with love and attention to detail!

2. Do you accept custom orders?

Absolutely! We love bringing your ideas to life. If you have a specific color, size, or design in mind, just get in touch with me and we'll work together to create something special.

3. How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Since each item is handmade, production time may vary depending on the item and order volume. Generally, orders take 1-2 weeks to ship, but we'll always keep you updated on the progress.

4. What materials do you use?

We use high-quality yarns in a variety of fibers, including cotton, wool, and acrylic. Each product is crafted to be durable, soft, comfortable, ensuring long-lasting quality.

5. Can I return or exchange my order?

We take great pride in our work and want you to be happy with your purchase! If you're not fully satisfied, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order to discuss returns or exchanges.

6. How do I care for my crochet items?

To keep your crochet pieces looking their best, we recommend hand washing with cold water and laying them flat to dry. Each item may have specific care instructions, whichh will be included with your order.
